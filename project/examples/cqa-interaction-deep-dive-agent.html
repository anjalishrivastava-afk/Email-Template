Hi Vinayaka,

Every one of your customer calls is scored by CQA. But did you know you can see exactly why each call received the score it did? The Interaction Deep Dive page gives you complete transparency into every score.

What is the Interaction Deep Dive?

When you click on any call in the Analysis List, you open its full detail view. This is where you go beyond the score and understand the reasoning behind it — with the recording, transcript, scorecard, and AI reasoning all in one place.

What can you do with it?

1 Listen to the recording — Play back the full call directly in the platform, no downloads needed
2 Read the transcript — See the complete conversation in text, so you can follow along with what was said
3 Review the scorecard — See scores broken down by category and sub-category, and expand any section to view individual KPI results
4 Understand the AI reasoning — Click any KPI to see exactly which parts of the conversation led to a pass or fail, so you know precisely what happened
5 Raise disputes — Disagree with a KPI score? Click “Raise Dispute”, add your reasoning, and your supervisor will review it
